VANGUARD MASTER CHANGELOG
-------------------------
v4.10.0 - Shift Tracking & Wake Lock (Current Build)
- `tt_shifts` table added to CoreDB. Generates Shift IDs tracking start/end times and GPS breadcrumbs.
- Agent Dashboard (`agent.html`) now defaults to a locked "PUNCH IN" overlay state on load if no active shift is open.
- Sidebar updated: "Log Out" replaced with "🛑 End Shift".
- `AgentCtrl` integrated with native `navigator.wakeLock.request('screen')` to force the tablet/phone screen to stay awake while the shift is active.
- Automated Background Breadcrumbs: While a shift is open, VanGuard grabs the GPS coordinates directly from Leaflet's marker every 30 seconds and pushes them into the `tt_shifts` array.
- Job Submission stamping logic updated: Every time `CoreDB.pushJob()` is called, it actively queries for an OPEN shift and permanently binds the `shiftId` to the job payload.

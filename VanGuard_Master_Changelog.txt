VANGUARD MASTER CHANGELOG
-------------------------
v4.12.0 - Agent HUD & Embedded Queue (Current Build)
- Agent Sidebar Job Bank: Replaced the old modal button with a live list embedded directly into the sidebar. Jobs explicitly ASSIGNED to the worker have a green border, OPEN POOL jobs have a yellow border.
- Map Jumping & Geocoding: Clicking a job in the sidebar parses the text through OpenStreetMap, pans the map instantly to the location, and drops a temporary marker.
- Accept Job Workflow: Clicking an Open Pool marker offers an "Accept Job" button. Clicking it instantly registers the job to that specific worker and removes it from the global pool.
- Native Routing Link: Added a "Route 📍" button to the job popup which triggers standard Google/Apple map URIs for driving directions.
- Agent HUD (Van Click): Added a click listener to the `userMarker` (van icon). It opens a live HUD showing Shift Time, Distance Traveled (Haversine formula applied dynamically to the GPS breadcrumbs array), and Job Counts.
- Agent Shift History: A new modal overlay attached to the HUD allows the agent to instantly pull up the site name, SRN, and notes of any job they completed during the current shift.

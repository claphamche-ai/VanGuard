VanGuard is a centralized, real-time field operations platform designed to track, manage, and dispatch asset maintenance teams. Utilizing live GPS telematics, rapid field reporting, and geographic data correlation, this portal provides comprehensive oversight of city-wide infrastructure preservation and rapid response efforts.
